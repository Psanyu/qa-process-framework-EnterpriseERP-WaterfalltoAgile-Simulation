## 1. Project Title
Enterprise Procurement & Inventory Management System (EPIMS)

---

## 2. Business Objective

The organization aims to implement a centralized Enterprise Resource Planning (ERP) solution to streamline procurement and inventory workflows.

Key goals:

- Eliminate duplicate vendor records
- Improve purchase order traceability
- Enable real-time inventory updates
- Ensure financial reconciliation accuracy
- Support audit compliance and reporting

---

## 3. Problem Statement

The legacy system relied on manual approvals and disconnected databases, leading to:

- Data inconsistencies between warehouse and finance
- Delayed purchase approvals
- Invoice mismatches
- Lack of transaction traceability

---

## 4. Scope

### In Scope
- Vendor Management
- Purchase Order Creation & Approval
- Goods Receipt (GRN)
- Inventory Updates
- 3-Way Invoice Matching
- Financial Posting

### Out of Scope
- Payroll processing
- HR module
- External banking gateway integration

---

## 5. Stakeholders

- Procurement Manager
- Warehouse Supervisor
- Finance Lead
- Business Analyst
- QA Team
- Development Team

---

## 6. Assumptions

- System will be web-based.
- Backend database will use Oracle.
- Role-based access control will be implemented.
- Daily builds will be provided during testing phase.

---

## 7. Success Criteria

- 95% test case pass rate before release
- No Critical or High defects open at production sign-off
- Inventory and finance data reconciliation accuracy > 99%
